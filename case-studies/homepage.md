## About Section (Homepage Summary)

I'm a UX Designer with nearly five years of end-to-end product design experience, currently embedded across two product teams at Pets at Home: one focused on e-commerce conversion for web and app, and the other on an enterprise Android platform used by over 5,000 store colleagues across 450+ stores. That breadth means I'm comfortable working on consumer-facing conversion problems and complex internal tooling in the same week.

My approach is data-driven and pragmatic. I use research, analytics, and facilitated discovery to reduce risk and validate direction, but I don't treat process as a performance or reach for workshops and testing unless they'll actually change a decision.
